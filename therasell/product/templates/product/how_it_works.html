How It Works

For Buyers :

1. If you see something you like, contact the seller in the listing with any questions before you buy.

2. Pay for your item securely through Paypal and select shipping and the seller will ship the item to you. Your funds will only be released once you receive your item.

Or

3. Pay for your item securely through Paypal and select the Pick Up/Delivery option.

The Address of the seller will be released with payment.

Message the seller for pick up options.

Always meet at a safe space if picking up!

Accept your item at delivery or pick up. Your funds will only then be released.

4. If there is an issue with your purchase, you can file a dispute by emailing us at therasell100@gmail.com, within 3 days of receiving the item


For Sellers:

1. Create a free account and set up free listings for your items. It's easy and fast!

2. Sell to other businesses or consumers looking for therapeutic and medical equipment equipment.

3. Ship your item or meet at a safe spot for delivery or pick up.

4. Get paid!

Our fees are very simple and straightforward. For all sales under $15, TheraSELL takes a flat commission of $2.95. You keep the rest. For sales of $15 or more, you keep 90% of your sale and TheraSELL commission is 10%. Once your sale has been delivered and received by your buyer, the earnings from your sale are yours.

If you would like to donate your item we take NO COMMISSION! Shipping costs will be passed to the buyer!

You are protected every time you place an order on TheraSELL. When you purchase an item on TheraSELL, we keep your payment safe until you tell us you've received your order. If your item does not match the description, please open a case within 3 days of delivery, before the order is marked as received.
